Inspired by GitHub, CoLab is a cross-discipline project collaboration platform for college and university courses. It will allow students to complete group projects online, view their classmates' additions and revisions and revert to previous versions. Teachers, in turn, will be able to monitor their students' progress and evaluate the contributions of each group member. Each project will also have a bulletin board to facilitiate communication and keep a record of decisions made and ideas shared.
